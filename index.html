Our Story

For over 35 years, Moldart has set the benchmark for quality and technical precision across two distinct industrial ecosystems — wood and steel.

Built on the principle of integrating European quality with the efficiency of global supply chains and the cost advantage of Indian operations, we deliver a unique value proposition to manufacturers, architects, and industrialists.

Supply Chain
WOOD ECOSYSTEM

An integrated supply chain spanning precision lamination tooling (press plates, press pads), décor/process inputs, engineered panel substrates, and finished architectural products including flooring and furniture.

STEEL DIVISION

A specialized metallurgical division focused on industrial press plates for surface-critical applications (electronics, security) and premium decorative stainless steel for architectural and industrial use.

01 / Raw Materials

LAMINATION & DÉCOR INPUTS

PRESS PLATES

  • Grades: SS 304 · SS 420 · SS 630
  • Core Hardness: ~40–45 HRC
  • Chrome Surface: 65–70 HRC
  • Texture fidelity & wear resistance

PRESS PADS

  • Silicone–Copper composite
  • ~80,000–100,000 cycles
  • Max width: 3300 mm
  • Uniform heat & pressure

ENGRAVED CYLINDERS

  • Precision rotogravure
  • Engraving depth: 18–25 μm
  • Roughness Ra: 0.2–0.3 μm
  • HD pattern transfer

PRINTED DÉCOR PAPER

  • Weight: 60–85 GSM
  • Wet Tensile: >6N
  • Melamine impregnation
  • Wood grain & pattern designs
02 / Semi-Finished

ENGINEERED SUBSTRATES

PLYWOOD

  • Density 500–700 kg/m³
  • Thickness 3–40 mm
  • Shear strength ≥1.5 MPa
  • High-strength structural

OSB

  • Density 600–750 kg/m³
  • Thickness 6–25 mm
  • Bond strength ≥0.35 N/mm²
  • Load-bearing construction

FIBERBOARD

  • MDF/HDF 720–1,000 kg/m³
  • Thickness 2–25 mm
  • Smooth for lamination
  • Painting or overlay ready

PARTICLEBOARD

  • Density 600–750 kg/m³
  • Thickness 4–44 mm
  • Grades: E0 / E1 / CARB
  • Standard furniture core
